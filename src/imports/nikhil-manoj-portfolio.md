Nikhil Manoj

Product Designer | Systems Thinker | Digital Product Strategist

Nikhil Manoj is a Product Designer with a foundation in Computer Science and Engineering and professional experience in UI/UX design. With four years of academic training in engineering and industry experience at Flxiidesk Workspaces in Kochi, he combines technical fluency with structured design thinking. He is currently pursuing his MDes in Product Design at the National Institute of Design, Ahmedabad.

His work focuses on designing scalable digital systems that simplify complex problems. Drawing from his engineering background, he approaches product design as system architecture, not surface styling. He is particularly interested in ambiguous problem spaces where defining the problem is as critical as solving it.

Across his projects, he moves from research synthesis and system modeling to interaction design and prototyping, grounding decisions in feasibility, usability, and long-term coherence.

UPI Balance Visibility Intervention

Within India’s UPI payment ecosystem, users often experience uncertainty immediately after completing a transaction due to the absence of contextual account balance visibility. Nikhil identified this friction point and proposed integrating balance visibility directly into the “Payment Successful” screen.

The intervention reduces cognitive load, improves trust during a critical micro-moment, and minimizes the need for additional navigation. The solution demonstrates micro-interaction thinking, behavioral insight, and ecosystem-aware integration within an existing financial product flow.

LocalAI

LocalAI is a privacy-centered AI platform enabling users to run language models locally instead of relying on cloud infrastructure. The project explores how complex distributed systems can be made accessible and transparent to end users.

Nikhil designed the experience around simplified model management, system resource visibility, and clear privacy boundaries. The product reframes AI infrastructure as an understandable, controllable user system rather than a black-box service.

This work reflects platform thinking, technical systems awareness, and product clarity under computational constraints.

Nirmaan

Financial Engine & Pricing Simulator for Entrepreneurs

Nirmaan is a financial simulation engine designed to help early-stage entrepreneurs visualize profitability before market launch. The system allows users to input manufacturing costs, overheads, margins, and pricing strategies to dynamically simulate profit outcomes and break-even thresholds.

The project demonstrates structured systems modeling, financial logic translation into intuitive interfaces, and decision-support design for non-financial users. It positions product design as strategic enablement rather than interface decoration.

FluxKey

Adaptive Interaction Console with Haptic Smart Dial

FluxKey is an advanced programmable macro interface integrating a haptic torque-based smart dial with contextual display feedback. Designed as a precision interaction system, it enables adaptive control workflows across digital applications.

The project explores embedded interaction design, firmware logic, context-aware input mapping, and hardware-software integration. It demonstrates end-to-end product execution, from conceptual modeling to a functional prototype.
